Overview

The Training and Placement Cell, School of Studies of Engineering and Technology (T&P Cell, SOSET) of Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya, Bilaspur, is dedicated to facilitating student placements across various disciplines, including engineering, management, and sciences. The T&P Cell, SOSET collaborates with leading organizations to provide internship and training opportunities, ensuring students are career-ready.

The placement season runs from late July to March, during which numerous companies conduct campus interviews. The T&P Cell, SOSET is equipped with modern facilities for group discussions and interviews, and it organizes pre-placement talks to foster interaction between students and potential employers.

A committee of student representatives from undergraduate and postgraduate programs supports the T&P Cell, SOSET in developing annual policies and guidelines for the placement process. This collaboration ensures that student feedback is integral to decision-making.

Key Activities of the T&P Cell, SOSET:

Offering communication skill development from the first semester.

Skill Development Programs

Organizing mock interviews & aptitude tests for pre-final & final-year students.

Facilitating interactions between students & industry professionals.
