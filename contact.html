How to Reach GGV

About Bilaspur

Bilaspur, a charming city nestled in the heart of Chhattisgarh, is known for its warm hospitality and rich heritage. It offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition, making it an ideal place for students to thrive academically and personally.

Travel By Air

The nearest airport to GGV is Swami Vivekananda Airport in Raipur, located about 110 km from Bilaspur.
Direct flights are available from major cities such as Delhi, Mumbai, and Kolkata.

Travel By Rail

Bilaspur is well connected by rail with two major railway stations:
Bilaspur Junction: Located 12-13 km from GGV.
Uslapur Railway Station: Approximately 10 km from GGV.

Travel By Road

Bilaspur has an extensive road network with regular bus services from major cities in Chhattisgarh and neighboring states.
