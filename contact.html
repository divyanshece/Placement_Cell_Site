How to Reach GGV

About Bilaspur

Bilaspur, a charming city nestled in the heart of Chhattisgarh, is known for its warm hospitality and rich heritage. It offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition, making it an ideal place for students to thrive academically and personally. Whether you're visiting for academic purposes or simply exploring, Bilaspur promises an enriching experience.

Travel By Air

The nearest airport to GGV is Swami Vivekananda Airport in Raipur, located about 110 km from Bilaspur. Direct flights are available from major cities such as Delhi, Mumbai, and Kolkata.

From Raipur Airport, you can hire a taxi (INR 2,500 - INR 3,000) or take a bus to Bilaspur. Alternatively, Bilaspur also has a small domestic airport with limited flight connections.

Travel By Rail

Bilaspur is well connected by rail with two major railway stations:

  • Bilaspur Junction: Located 12-13 km from GGV.
  • Uslapur Railway Station: Approximately 10 km from GGV.

From both stations, auto-rickshaws and taxis are readily available. Fares range from INR 150-400 depending on the type of vehicle.

Travel By Road

Bilaspur has an extensive road network with regular bus services from major cities in Chhattisgarh and neighboring states. Both state-run and private buses operate routes to Bilaspur.

You can reach Bilaspur from Raipur by bus or taxi in approximately 3 hours. Taxi fares range between INR 2,500 and INR 3,500.
