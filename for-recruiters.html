Procedures & Policies

Our Central Placement Cell follows structured procedures to ensure smooth & transparent placement activities. For detailed policies & regulations, please go through our placement policy documentation or contact us directly.

Placement Policy (for company)

1: Registration (JIF/IIF)

Companies submit a Job/Internship Invitation Form (JIF/IIF) with job details, after an formal invitation from CPC.

2: Profile Verification

CPC verifies job profiles & alignment with student qualifications before confirming with the company.

3: Pre-Placement Talk

CPC assigns slots to companies to conduct presentations/meeting, introducing role & company culture to students.

4: Selection Process

Eligible candidates go through assessment tests, group discussions, or rounds as required.

5: Shortlisting

Companies provide CPC with list of shortlisted & waitlisted candidates by a specified deadline.

6: Interview Scheduling

CPC schedules interview dates for companies to conduct in-person or virtual interviews with shortlisted students.

7: Final Selection List

Companies submit the final list of selected students by the end of their assigned interview slots.

8: Offer Letter Allotment

CPC distributes offer letters to selected candidates, officially concluding the hiring process.

FAQs

How do I register for recruitment activities?

You can register by contacting the CPC directly or via our online registration form available on the GGV website.

What facilities are provided during campus visits?
